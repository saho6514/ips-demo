Why Change
Standing Still Has a Cost

Dealer loyalty data is invisible

Ansira never connects to the CRM. Co-op spend, relationship signals, and buying history don't reach sales, marketing, or service. The Advantage Program is their biggest dealer investment — and it's a black box.

The consumer site commoditizes the brand

A site built around keyword search and price sorting can't guide a shopper toward ADVAN or GEOLANDAR. Premium SKUs compete on a price list — not on merit, fit, or story.

Every custom build adds another silo

Rebuilt pricing engine. Separate dealer portal. RingCentral over SCV. Each internal decision compounds the integration gap — and the CIO's build bias means it will keep happening.

New segments will inherit today's fragmentation

OTR, ag, and mining expansion is happening now. No architecture decision is still a decision — these entities will be built on whatever foundation exists today.
