The Capability Gap
This use case is only possible when marketing can see Commerce Cloud purchase history and Revenue Cloud contract terms in real time. A disconnected marketing tool sees none of this. It cannot know a distributor is approaching a reorder window. It cannot pull live contract pricing. It cannot connect an email send to downstream revenue. This is the gap.
